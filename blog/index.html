Welcome to the Deeziner blog

Hello… welcome to my blog. I plan to use this blog as a kind of diary and a place to go for other creative types like me.

I am a professional graphic designer and musician. I like to dabble in all kinds of multi media projects ranging from Web Design, Animation, 3D Models, 2D Games, and Songwriting.

So, whether it’s fonts that I think you should know about or software that can help you get your project done, I plan to blog about my latest project (whatever it may be at the time) and let you know what tools and tips I have discovered each week.
