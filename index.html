I have a Bachelor of Arts in Applied Graphics Technology with First Class Hons from The Glasgow Caledonian University. I am skilled in Web Design (HTML & CSS), Print Design (Photoshop, Illustrator & InDesign) and Animation (After Effects, Maya, Flash).
